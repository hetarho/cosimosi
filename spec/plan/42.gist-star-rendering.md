# 42. gist star rendering

> Gist-star rendering — the presentation unit of Epic E: it makes the **z-axis 2-layer universe** ([V9]) visible as 3D
> depth and renders **gist stars rising into the neocortical layer** as a memory's `semantic_stage` advances ([C6]). A
> gist star is a read-only neocortical body at the memory's `(x, y)` but a higher `z` ([C6][I5]); clicking it opens
> ViewSemantic ([R8], plan 34). The consolidation "pulled-upward" replay choreography ([V8][C8]) is a **reserved slot**
> the plan-31 acceleration plays into — booked here, not built.
> Scope: FE | Status: planning
>
> Layer P (presentation) — the third and final unit of Epic E, over the built read side (Epic A) and the Epic E domain
> ([40.semanticization](40.semanticization.md)) + use-case ([41.consolidation-usecase](41.consolidation-usecase.md)). Home:
> a new `entities/gist-star` visual entity + a neocortical-layer refinement of `widgets/universe-canvas`
> ([23.universe-canvas](23.universe-canvas.md)), mounting the plan-14/23 renderer. It fills the **neocortical-layer render
> + the z-depth split**; it leaves the semanticize model, the gist-timer, the coords, and the consolidation trigger to
> plan 40/41, and the replay choreography to a reserved slot.

## Purpose

Epic A renders every star in a single visual band and treats abstraction as *nothing yet* — [24.star-neuron-rendering](24.star-neuron-rendering.md)
explicitly deferred the z-axis gist and the neocortical layer ("abstraction is z-rise, not star shape"). Epic E's domain
([40.semanticization](40.semanticization.md)) now produces the missing facts: a memory's `semantic_stage` (0–4) and, per
risen stage, a **neocortical coordinate** — the same emergent `(x, y)` as the hippocampal star, copied upward with only
`z` raised into the neocortex band ([C6][I5]). What is still missing is the **rendering**: the universe page must show
its **two z-layers** as real 3D depth — the **hippocampus universe** (z 0–10; concrete episodic stars + gray latent
stars) below, and the **neocortex universe** (z 15–25; gist versions) above — and must render a **gist star** for each
risen stage at that neocortical coordinate.

This unit builds that: the **`entities/gist-star`** visual entity — a neocortical body projecting a memory's semantic
stage — and a `widgets/universe-canvas` refinement so the two bands read as depth a user navigates ([V0][V9]). A gist
star is **read-only**: clicking it opens ViewSemantic ([R8], plan 34), never 회고하기 (the episodic star's, plan 33/36).
And it **books the consolidation-replay slot** ([V8][C8]): the "pulled-upward" stage-rise and the sequential relate-star
replay are a reserved slot the plan-31 acceleration plays into once authored — this unit exposes the slot and plays a
**neutral** rise, it does not build the full replay. It builds the **presentation only**; the semanticize model, the
gist-timer, the z-coords, and the advance-triggered consolidation are the Epic E domain/use-case's.

## Scope / Non-goals

**In scope**
- **`entities/gist-star`** — a new **visual (rendering) entity** projecting the neocortical gist version of an
  `episodic-memory` through the plan-14 asset-source port (`VisualBodySource`), importing the domain mirror **only via
  `@x`** (`entities/gist-star/@x/episodic-memory`), exactly as the plan-24 visual entities do (ARCHITECTURE §3.4). One
  gist star is rendered **per risen semantic stage** at that stage's neocortical coordinate.
  - **Position = the hippocampal `(x, y)`, raised into the neocortex z-band** ([C6][I5][V9]). The gist star copies the
    episodic star's emergent `(x, y)` and sits at a `z` inside `force_sim.neocortex_z_*`; the **neocortex layer has no
    force-sim** ([C6] — neocortical stability), so this unit places the body at the coord the read model feeds and never
    runs a sim for it.
  - **Appearance channels** reuse plan 24's grammar with an abstraction reading: **color = the memory's primary
    emotion** via the single `mood → color` palette seam (plan 17) — a gist keeps the memory's emotion ([M3][I3]);
    **size = `EffectiveStrength`** (plan 18, read-time). Abstraction is expressed by **z-rise (higher stage → higher z /
    deeper band) + a distinct gist look** (softer/more diffuse than the sharp episodic body), **not** by star shape
    ([V5] — shape is the episodic seed; abstraction is z, not seed). **Decided (2026-07):** the gist body is a **distinct
    diffuse TSL body** (its own asset-source), not the episodic seed-body reparameterized — abstraction reads as "the
    same memory, gistified," and the shape channel stays the episodic seed's alone.
- **Neocortical-layer refinement of `widgets/universe-canvas`** ([23.universe-canvas](23.universe-canvas.md)) — the two
  z-bands render as **navigable 3D depth** ([V0][V9]): hippocampus (z 0–10; episodic stars + latent field) below, a
  visible gap, neocortex (z 15–25; gist stars) above. This unit **refines** the existing scene (adds the gist layer + the
  depth read), it does **not** remount the renderer, the force-sim worker, the camera rig, or the `GetUniverse` read.
- **Gist star → ViewSemantic ([R8])** — a gist star is **clickable and read-only**: selecting it opens the ViewSemantic
  read (plan 34) for that `(memory, stage)` — gist text viewing, no rewrite. This unit **emits the selection** (the
  `(memory_id, stage)` a gist body carries) and hands it to plan 34's viewer; it does not build the viewer, its cost
  gate, or its panel.
- **Consolidation-replay reserved slot ([V8][C8])** — the neutral **stage-rise motion** (a gist body appearing / rising
  into the neocortex over an advance interval) plays through the plan-31 acceleration seam; the full **"pulled-upward"**
  choreography and the sequential relate-star **replay** ([C2]) are a **reserved slot** this unit books but does not
  build. It exposes the per-interval stage-rise events on the same choreography seam plan 31 defined, so the authored
  replay drops in later without re-plumbing.
- **web (open beta) + mobile (MVP) parity** — the same `entities/gist-star` + canvas refinement on both apps, one TSL
  source; `*.native.tsx` only where a canvas host primitive genuinely differs (ARCHITECTURE §3.5). Mobile MVP may lower
  gist-instance counts / drop post-FX; the projection + body are shared verbatim.

**Non-goals**
- **No semanticize domain, no gist-timer, no z-coords.** `Semanticize`, the abstraction-stage model, the gist-timer
  ([C6a]), and the neocortical coordinate derivation ([C6]: copy x,y, raise z) are [40.semanticization](40.semanticization.md).
  This unit **renders** the `semantic_stage` + coords the read model carries; it computes none of them.
- **No consolidation trigger, no replay reorg, no downscaling.** `Consolidate` on advance, replay-reorg ([C2]), and
  `Downscale` (SHY [C4][I9]) are [41.consolidation-usecase](41.consolidation-usecase.md), fired by the AdvanceProgression
  hook (Epic B). This unit only plays the **visual** stage-rise and reserves the replay slot.
- **No ViewSemantic read / cost / panel.** The gist-text read, its Twinkle spend gate ([R8][G4]), and the viewer panel are
  [34.view-semantic-usecase](34.view-semantic-usecase.md) / [35.star-detail-panel](35.star-detail-panel.md). This unit
  only makes a gist star **clickable** and forwards the selection.
- **No recall / reconsolidation.** 회고하기 is the **episodic** star's ([R1], plan 33/36); a gist star is read-only
  ([R8]). Gist stars are never reconsolidation targets and this unit exposes no rewrite affordance on them.
- **No forgetting visuals.** Brightness dimming + word-loss on the *episodic* star are [39.forgetting-visuals](39.forgetting-visuals.md);
  gist is the **independent abstraction axis** ([F]≠[C]). This unit does not dim or word-strip a gist star.
- **No new RPC.** `semantic_stage` is already on the `GetUniverse` DTO (plan 23 read); the neocortical coords are added to
  that read by [40.semanticization](40.semanticization.md). This unit adds no proto.
- No hardcoded tuning scalars in FE (visual ranges live in `values.yaml`; shader graphs + palette stay code/content).

## Design

### Placement (FSD, §3.4/§3.1)
```
apps/web/src/entities/gist-star/
├── @x/episodic-memory.ts   the one-way @x import of the plan-23 domain mirror (semantic_stage + neocortical coords)
├── ui/                     the gist-star body layer (consumes the 3d-renderer asset-source port; no three)
├── model/                  pure stage→body projection (channels + z placement) — shared verbatim web+mobile
└── index.ts                public API (the layer + the (memory_id, stage) selection event)

packages/3d-renderer/src/assets/bodies/gist-star-body.ts   NEW diffuse gist VisualBodySource (three confined here)
```
`entities/gist-star` sits beside `entities/{star,cell-star,filament}` (plan 24) as a fourth **visual** entity; its only
upstream domain dependency is the `@x` public API of the plan-23 `episodic-memory` mirror. The gist body is authored in
the `3d-renderer` package (like the star/cell-star/filament bodies) so `three` stays confined to the package; the entity
`ui` consumes the package layer, never `three`. `widgets/universe-canvas` composes the new layer into its existing
`UniverseScene` and feeds it the neocortical coords, exactly as it feeds the episodic layer its hippocampal coords. The
same slices exist on mobile; `model` + the body are shared verbatim, and only a canvas host primitive forks via
`*.native.tsx` (§3.5).

### The z-axis 2-layer universe as 3D depth ([V9][V0])
The universe page already renders in 3D ([V0], plan 23) with standard zoom/rotate/pan. This unit makes the **two z-bands
legible as depth**:
- **Hippocampus universe** — z within `force_sim.hippocampus_z_min…hippocampus_z_max` (0–10): the concrete episodic stars
  (plan 24) + the gray latent field (plan 25). Force-sim owns its `(x, y, z)` (plan 19).
- **Neocortex universe** — z within `force_sim.neocortex_z_min…neocortex_z_max` (15–25): the gist stars, above a visible
  gap. **No force-sim** ([C6]): each gist body is placed at its fed coord, stable.
- The bands are the **same 3D space**, navigated as depth — this unit does **not** add a mode toggle or a second camera;
  it renders both bands in the one scene and lets the plan-23 camera rig fly between them. A subtle depth cue (band-level
  fog/haze or a faint boundary plane at the gap) may distinguish the layers — **Decided (2026-07):** the depth cue is a
  restrained rendering affordance (fog gradient across the gap), **not** a hard wall; the gap is `force_sim`'s existing
  z-band values (`force_sim.hippocampus_z_max`=10 to `force_sim.neocortex_z_min`=15 — z 10–15 is the empty gap), no new coordinate config.

### `entities/gist-star` — the neocortical gist body
One gist star **per risen semantic stage** of a memory (a memory at `semantic_stage = 2` shows **2** gist stars — stages
1 and 2 — since risen stages persist, [C7]). Each gist body is a pure projection of stored facts carried by the
`episodic-memory` mirror (from the `GetUniverse` DTO, plan 23, extended by plan 40):

| Channel | Source | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **position** | hippocampal `(x, y)` copied + neocortical `z` (plan 40 coord) | z rises with stage within the neocortex band; no force-sim ([C6][I5]) |
| **color** | memory primary emotion → `mood → color` palette seam (plan 17) | a gist keeps its memory's emotion ([M3][I3]); palette-agnostic |
| **size** | `EffectiveStrength` (plan 18, read-time) | same accumulation grammar as the episodic star ([V3]) |
| **look** | a distinct **diffuse** TSL body (its own asset-source) | abstraction reads as "gistified," not a new seed-shape ([V5]) |

- **Abstraction = z + look, never shape.** The episodic **seed** shape channel is **not** reused for abstraction ([V5]);
  the gist body is a separate diffuse form, and deeper stages sit at a higher z (and read progressively more diffuse). A
  higher stage does **not** change the memory's emotion color or the seed of its episodic star.
- **One-way projection (§3.4).** The gist entity reads the domain mirror via `@x`, the palette seam (plan 17), and
  `EffectiveStrength` (plan 18), and produces a body through the asset-source port. It writes **no** visual word back into
  the domain slice or the `api` mapper, and it never reverse-projects the neocortical coordinate ([I5]).
- **Instanced (§3.3).** Gist bodies are one (or bucketed) instanced mesh of the diffuse TSL body; per-instance
  size/color/z ride instance attributes/uniforms; coordinates are read per frame in `useFrame`, never React state. Reuses
  the plan-14 `rendering.instance_bucket_size`.

### Gist star → ViewSemantic ([R8]) — clickable, read-only
A gist body carries its `(memory_id, semantic_stage)`. Selecting it **emits a ViewSemantic selection** (through the
entity's public API) that [34.view-semantic-usecase](34.view-semantic-usecase.md) consumes to open the gist-text viewer
for that `(memory, stage)`. A gist star is **read-only**: there is **no** 회고하기 on it ([R8] — recall/reconsolidation
is the episodic star's, plan 33/36), and this unit exposes no rewrite path. The Twinkle spend gate on viewing ([R8][G4])
is plan 34/F's; this unit only makes the body clickable and forwards the selection.

### The consolidation-replay reserved slot ([V8][C8]) — book it, play neutral
On an advance interval `[previous, current]` (Epic B's clock moved, [41.consolidation-usecase](41.consolidation-usecase.md)
advanced some stages), the plan-31 acceleration plays. This unit contributes the **gist stage-rise** into that
choreography:
- **Neutral rise (built here).** A newly risen stage's gist body **appears / rises** into the neocortex band over the
  interval — a restrained, one-way ([I10]) upward motion. This is the minimum that makes a stage rise **felt**, on the
  same choreography seam plan 31 exposed (the interval + a per-interval event list, read via `getSnapshot()` in
  `useFrame`, §3.2). An **empty interval** (past-dated launch, view-only) plays **nothing**.
- **Reserved (not built).** The full **"pulled-upward" motion** ([C8]) and the **sequential relate-star replay** ([C2] —
  related stars re-firing/re-arranging during the sleep) are the **reserved slot** ([V8]). This unit books it: it emits
  per-interval stage-rise events on the choreography seam so the authored replay drops in later without re-plumbing, and
  it plays the neutral rise until then. **Decided (2026-07):** the replay slot is **owned by this plan's seam** but the
  *choreography content* (which stars replay, the sweep timing) lands with a later authored job — enumerated here as a
  reserved visual, matching plan 31's [V8] discipline.

### Golden-parity stance
This unit renders; it **shares no cross-runtime math**. The read-time functions it consumes (`EffectiveStrength`,
plan 18; the `mood → color` palette, plan 17) are already golden-parity where they need to be. The **z-coordinate math**
(copy x,y, raise z per stage) is [40.semanticization](40.semanticization.md)'s and is the golden-parity concern there —
this unit consumes the coord the read model feeds and adds no parallel computation, so no new golden-parity surface is
introduced here (matching plan 24's precedent: the visual layer holds no domain math).

### Web + mobile parity (§3.5)
One TSL diffuse-gist body serves both platforms (WGSL on web WebGPU, GLSL on the WebGL2 fallback, the RN WebGPU host on
mobile). The `entities/gist-star` `model` (projection) is pure/shared; only a canvas host primitive forks via
`*.native.tsx`. Mobile MVP may lower gist-instance counts / drop post-FX — **confirmed at implement via on-device RN
profiling** (decided 2026-07) — without changing the shared body or projection.
